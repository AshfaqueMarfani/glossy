About Glossy

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Glossy was born out of a passion for clean, effective cosmetics that celebrate natural beauty.

Our founder, Emma Reynolds, was frustrated with the beauty industry's focus on covering up rather than enhancing natural features. With a background in cosmetic chemistry and a vision for more transparent beauty products, she created Glossy to offer high-quality cosmetics that are kind to skin and the planet.

Our Values

Clean Beauty

We formulate without harmful chemicals, focusing on ingredients that nourish your skin while providing beautiful results.

Sustainability

From our recyclable packaging to our cruelty-free testing, we're committed to minimizing our environmental footprint.

Inclusivity

Beauty comes in all colors, shapes, and sizes. Our products are designed to celebrate diversity and enhance natural beauty.

Our Commitment

At Glossy, we're committed to creating products that make you look and feel your best without compromising on ethics or quality. We believe that makeup should be a tool for self-expression and confidence, not a mask to hide behind.

We promise:

  • To use only clean, safe ingredients in all our products
  • To never test on animals or use animal-derived ingredients
  • To continuously improve our environmental practices
  • To listen to our customers and evolve with their needs
  • To be transparent about our ingredients and manufacturing processes
